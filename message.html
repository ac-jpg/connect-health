Our SMS messaging system is an essential tool for modern healthcare practices, providing patient satisfaction and improving business operations. Patients are automatically notified of information on their loved ones and family members are no longer required to sit in waiting rooms, increasing overall patient satisfaction.

Clinical Alerts

With our service you can customize automatic messages for each of your patients, ensuring that they take necessary medications on time and take proper care of their health. We make it possible for you to set reminders at a certain time of day or week, with any pattern, depending on each patient's prescriptions or daily actions they need to do to maintain their health. Not only does this ensure better health for your patients, but it also increases patient satisfaction.

Manage

Our automatic messaging service can be customized to communicate with employees by sending individual, customized messages to staff members for shift reminders, emergency alerts, and opportunities to fill open shifts. Not only does this help organize things for your patients, but it also does so for the people working in your practice, ensuring that everything runs smoothly for in-person staff/doctors and those who work virtually.

Reasons to Use

Our automatic messaging feature provides you with the ease and convenience to connect with your patients according to your needs. In this increasingly digital world, the more you can automate, the more effective your practice can be and the easier it will be for your patients.

Increased Engagement

Our automatic messaging feature offers consistent returns and increased engagement from your patients by keeping them up to date on appointment reminders and information with an effective automated system. A major obstacle faced by medical practices now is reminding patients for appointment times and having patients remember to follow medical advice daily; our service remedies this through scheduled updates designed specifically for your practice.

Easy Setup

We believe in simple and efficient tele-health tools that make keeping up with your patients as easy as possible. That is why our automatic messaging feature is simple to setup for both you and your patients, and gives you increased control over features so you can customize it to be as complex as you need it to be. This allows you to focus on other parts of running your practice by letting our services handle patient engagement and let you put your attention where it is needed.

Flexible Options

We offer a wide array of features and functionality for you to use with our automatic messaging service. The features are built with tools that are flexible and can be adapted to your practice to be used however you prefer without needing to learn or use complex software. There are lots of different ways to send texts, but ConnectHealth prioritizes quality and only offers messaging routes that give your practice the best delivery.

Automated Responses

We offer automated workflows where you can create a personalized experience for each patient that tailors the content of the messages based on their needs. You can customize it as you see fit based on time since last sent, whether a patient has viewed a message, or the patient's response. We offer a special time-based feature that sends texts automatically after your patient begins using our service and allows you to create a series of messages that can be setup all at once.

Easy to Manage

You can manage all of your automated messages from one location with complete control over all settings and features.
