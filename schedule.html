How it Works

Our online appointment booking system delivers notifications to physicians and patients while allowing staff to easily confirm and book appointments. As a bonus, it's available 24/7 and provides an instant solution to increase your revenue.

Reschedule

Make it easy for your patients to alter their appointments and modify their medical calendar with just one click. All the available dates and time slots are accessible without the need for constant staff assistance or to go through the entire process of booking another appointment.

Staff Accessibility

When scheduling appointments, our system considers insurance, availability, type, location, age, history, and more. Our scheduling service utilizes advanced technology to make decisions easy and self-sufficient so patients can go through this process effortlessly.

Never Miss an Appointment

Our service provides desktop notifications, email reminders, and text reminders to ensure that you and your patients never miss an appointment. If there is a scheduling conflict, you can request a rescheduled meeting through our solution as well.

Why Choose Online Scheduling

According to recent studies, 67% of the patients surveyed preferred online scheduling, especially self-scheduling. 90% tend to choose their healthcare provider based on conveniences like texting and ease of scheduling and rescheduling. Our solution accounts for all the in-person choices that have to be made by staff or patients while making it easy for you to give your patients an organized scheduling system, effectively reducing your no-show percent and increasing patient interaction.
