Medical Record Database

Keep track of all of your patients’ files in a customizable digital database that is both intuitive to use and easy to integrate with your existing database.

Why You Need This

Allows Quick Access to Patient’s Records
With our solution, it only requires a few clicks of the mouse to pull up a patient’s medical record.
Stays Up to Date
With both your practice and your patients (to a more limited extent) having the ability to update the database, patients’ records are sure to stay current and accurate.

Our Novel Dual-System

Long-Term

The long-term features of the database house all of your patients’ medical records including their medical history, previous prescriptions, etc. Our feature allows your practice to easily and quickly update this data. We also allow patients to update certain aspects of their history when they need to (your practice can determine which aspects of their medical record the patients can update).

Short-Term

The short-term aspects of this feature allow patients to directly upload/send data about their current illness/injury/problem to the doctor. In other words, the short-term part of the database will hold the patient’s data for their soonest appointment. This allows the patient to send pictures of their injury/illness, their temperature reading, etc., making it easier for your doctors to effectively help them. After the appointment, you can easily transfer the necessary information to the long-term portion of the database

Security

We have several security measures set in place, from separate identification locks for each of your patients, to a secure pathway that protects all the data that is being sent and received. We also have a security protocol set in place to safeguard all your patients files and records, in both the short term and long term storage, thus, giving your practice and patients a 99% guarantee to a successful and trustworthy service.

Why is this beneficial?

By using our medical database, you get a live view of everything that has to do with your patients, from their medical history to instant updates on their health whenever you need it. You can then use this data to assess your patients' health, be it for a recent appointment or even to see if certain medication clashes with their medical history, and tailor your methods to better help them. There's no reason to worry about forgotten medical files, vaccine charts, or blood test results with our service. This service has it all on deck for you and your patients.
